Negroni

Description

The Negroni is a timeless cocktail that combines gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari with a hint of orange. It's a perfect aperitif with its intriguing blend of bitterness and sweetness, and its deep, vibrant red color.

Shaken/Stirred:

Stirred

Base Spirit:

Gin

Glass:

Old Fashioned Glass

Garnish:

Orange Peel

Ingredients

  • 30ml
  • 30ml
  • 30ml
  • Gin
  • Sweet Vermouth
  • Campari

Preparation

Mix gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari in an old fashioned glass filled with ice. Stir well to combine and chill. Garnish with an orange peel twist.
