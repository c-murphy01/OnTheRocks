Old Fashioned

Description

Originating in the early 19th century, the Old Fashioned is a timeless whiskey cocktail that melds the smooth, robust flavors of bourbon or rye with the sweetness of sugar and the zest of bitters, garnished traditionally with a twist of citrus. This iconic drink is revered for its classic simplicity and deep, comforting warmth.

Shaken/Stirred:

Stirred

Base Spirit:

Whiskey

Glass:

Rocks Glass

Garnish:

Orangle Peel

Ingredients

  • 60ml
  • 4-6 Dashes
  • 10ml
  • Bourbon
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Sugar Syrup

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice. Stir well, then strain and serve over ice in a rocks glass. Garnish with an orange peel.
