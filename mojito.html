Mojito

Description

Originating from Cuba, the Mojito is a refreshing cocktail that blends sweet, citrus, and mint flavors with rum, topped off with a splash of soda for a crisp, invigorating drink. This iconic concoction is perfect for savoring on a hot day or during lively summer evenings.

Shaken/Stirred:

Muddled/Stirred

Base Spirit:

Rum

Glass:

Highball Glass

Garnish:

Mint Sprig

Ingredients

  • 50ml
  • 20ml
  • 2 tsp
  • To top up
  • 5-6
  • White Rum
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Sugar
  • Soda Water
  • Mint Leaves

Preparation

Muddle mint leaves and sugar in a highball glass. Add lime juice and rum, fill with ice, top with soda water. Stir well and garnish with a mint sprig.
