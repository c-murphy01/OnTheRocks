Caipirinha

Description

The Caipirinha is Brazil's most famous cocktail, made with cachaça, sugar, and lime. It's a simple yet potent mix that offers a refreshing burst of sweet and tart flavors.

Muddled/Mixed:

Muddled

Base Spirit:

Cachaça

Glass:

Old Fashioned Glass

Garnish:

Lime Wedge

Ingredients

  • 50ml
  • 2 tsp
  • 1
  • Cachaça
  • Sugar
  • Lime, cut into pieces

Preparation

Muddle lime pieces and sugar in an old fashioned glass. Fill the glass with ice, add cachaça, stir well. Garnish with a lime wedge.
