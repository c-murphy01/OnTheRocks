Martini

Description

The Martini is the epitome of elegance, featuring gin and dry vermouth, typically garnished with an olive or lemon twist. Its simplicity belies a complex and timeless flavor profile that has made it a classic.

Shaken/Stirred:

Stirred

Base Spirit:

Gin

Glass:

Martini Glass

Garnish:

Olive/Lemon Twist

Ingredients

  • 60ml
  • 10ml
  • Gin
  • Dry Vermouth

Preparation

Stir gin and vermouth with ice in a mixing glass. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with an olive or lemon twist.
