Margarita

Description

Originating from Mexico, the margarita is a refreshing cocktail that blends the tangy taste of lime with the smoothness of tequila and a hint of sweetness from orange liqueur, typically served with a salted rim for an extra pop of flavor. This beloved drink offers a perfect balance of sweet, sour, and salty notes, making it a timeless choice for any occasion.

Shaken/Stirred:

Shaken

Base Spirit:

Tequila

Glass:

Margarita Glass

Garnish:

Lime Wedge & Salt Rim

Ingredients

  • 45ml
  • 30ml
  • 20ml
  • 5ml
  • Tequila
  • Orange Liqueur
  • Lime Juice
  • Sugar Syrup (Optional)

Preparation

Run a lime wedge along the rim of a margarita glass and dip the glass in salt to rim it. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well, then strain into your glass. Garnish with an lime wheel.
