Paper Plane

Description

The Paper Plane is a modern cocktail with a balanced blend of bourbon, Amaro, Aperol, and lemon juice, offering a complex, bittersweet flavor profile. Originally created in New York, this cocktail has soared in popularity for its delightful interplay of herbal and citrus notes.

Preparation

Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake well, then strain and serve straight up in a coupe glass. Garnish with an twist of lemon peel.
