Pina Colada

Description

The Pina Colada, a beloved tropical cocktail, combines the rich flavors of rum with coconut cream and pineapple juice to create a velvety, refreshing drink that captures the essence of summer any time of the year.

Shaken/Blended:

Blended

Base Spirit:

Rum

Glass:

Pina Colada Glass

Garnish:

Pineapple Slice and Cherry

Ingredients

  • 60ml
  • 30ml
  • 90ml
  • Rum
  • Coconut Cream
  • Pineapple Juice

Preparation

Blend rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice with ice until smooth. Pour into a chilled Pina Colada glass. Garnish with a slice of pineapple and a cherry.
