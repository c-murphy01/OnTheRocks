Manhattan

Description

A true classic, the Manhattan cocktail exudes elegance with its perfect blend of whiskey and sweet vermouth, enhanced by a dash of bitters. This drink is a staple at any sophisticated gathering.

Shaken/Stirred:

Stirred

Base Spirit:

Whiskey

Glass:

Martini Glass

Garnish:

Cherry

Ingredients

  • 50ml
  • 20ml
  • 2 Dashes
  • Whiskey
  • Sweet Vermouth
  • Angostura Bitters

Preparation

Stir whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters with ice in a mixing glass. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a cherry.
